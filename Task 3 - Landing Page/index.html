Popular Destinations

Explore our handpicked selection of India's most breathtaking destinations and create memories that last a lifetime.

Taj Mahal, Agra

Experience the breathtaking beauty of this iconic symbol of love. Marvel at the stunning white marble architecture and immerse yourself in Mughal history.

Kerala Backwaters

Cruise through serene waterways on traditional houseboats, surrounded by lush palm trees and peaceful villages in God's Own Country.

Rajasthan Palaces

Discover royal heritage in magnificent palaces and forts. Experience desert safaris and vibrant culture in the Land of Kings.

Varanasi Ghats

Witness spiritual ceremonies along the sacred Ganges River. Experience ancient traditions and cultural rituals in India's oldest living city.

Darjeeling Hills

Explore tea plantations with Himalayan views. Ride the historic toy train and experience the charm of this colonial hill station.

Goa Beaches

Relax on pristine beaches, explore Portuguese heritage, and enjoy vibrant nightlife in India's favorite coastal paradise.
